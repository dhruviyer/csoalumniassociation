Mentoring

All alumni are responsible for staying connected to the CSO program in some capacity. Alumni take on different mentorship roles based on seniority. Newer alumni work with the CSO cohort at their alma mater, acting as mentors and helping them bring their action plans to fruition. More experienced alumni are assigned to serve a regional CSO cabinets. In addition to these mentoring roles, alumni are often seen at CSO events acting as facilitators and volunteers.
