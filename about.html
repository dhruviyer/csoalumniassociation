About Us

Mission: "To keep CSO alumni engaged with the CSO program and foster a vibrant community of changemakers and leaders through mentorship, networking, and collective action"

Vision:
  1. We are leaders—we use our experience, knowledge, and character to help CSOs achieve their goals
  2. We are problem-solvers—we help CSOs overcome obstacles by using our empathy, compassion, and critical thinking
  3. We are changemakers—we use our resources and talents to positively impact the world around us
  4. We are mentors—we work to inspire and educate the next generation of CSOs
The CSO Alumni Association was founded in 2017 as a means to connect alumni Chief Science Officers with each other and the CSO program. A CSO alumnus is a student who has been awarded a certificate of graduation by the CSO program or has been approved to join the CSO alumni association by r ecommendation or invite. The association is run by CSO alumni and operates semiautonomously from the CSO program. The program is facilitated by an elected board of alumni, who oversee the different activities of the association.

The association sponsors three types of activities: mentorship, networking, and colelctive action. Mentorship activities connect the graduated CSOs with the active CSOs. New CSO alumni mentor their alma mater CSO team. Other alumni assist with regional cabinets and attend CSO events. CSOs also reunite for leadership summits and other networking events. Lastly, CSOs engagte in collective action projects to enhance STEM ecosystems around the country.
