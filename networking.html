Networking

The alumni association sponsors networking events that serve to connect alumni CSOs. These Alumni Summits bring together CSOs from around the country to explore ways to better improve the three pillars: mentorship, networking, and collective action. The events are a chance for board members to meet new alumni and set the year's agenda. These events are often run in parallel with CSO training institutes, so Alumni have a chance to network with the newest generation of CSOs.
