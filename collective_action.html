Collective Action

One of the cornerstones of the CSO program is a dedication to collective action. CSOs decide on the best method to achieve a goal that centers on improving the STEM ecosystem of a society, and CSO alumni are no different. Every year, the CSO alumni decide on a theme and headline collective action project, which all alumni CSOs contribute to collectively.
